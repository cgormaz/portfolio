Case Study

Rompechanchitos

Rompechanchitos (lit. piggy bank breaker) is an interactive guide about the current pension system in Chile and its consequences.

Play it on itchio

Background

"In Chile, half of the people who worked between 30 and 35 years, today receive a pension of less than the minimum wage. What awaits those of us who have not yet retired?"

The goal of Rompechanchitos was to spread easy-to-digest information about the failed Chilean pension system and why it needs to change.

Overview

Rompechanchitos was a solo project.

The guide was researched, written, and developed in two weeks. It also used the source code of Nicky Case's "The Evolution of Trust" as a base.
