Art Portfolio

Character Design

I've been working as a character designer since 2014, mostly for games but also for Vtubers and other kinds of projects. My process starts with a moodboard and client references, with which I then make initial sketches and later color variations.
I currently offer this service on the commission hub VGen.

Illustrations

Commercial character-based illustrations for games and Vtubers, as well as personal illustrations and fanarts.

Physical Merchandising

Prints, lenticular photocards, acrylic keychains, coloring books and more. These are sold at conventions and at my online store.
