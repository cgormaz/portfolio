About Me

Camila Gormaz

Game Developer & Artist

Indie developer & multidisciplinary artist, co-founder of BURA, with more than 8 years of professional experience in the industry. During that time she has worked mainly as an artist and designer for various commercial and personal projects, and her work has been awarded in different parts of the world.

Awards & Recognition

San Francisco, 2019

GDC #1ReasonToBe Panelist

#1ReasonToBe is one of the top rated talks of GDC. Started by Brenda Romero, and hosted by Rami Ismail, this panel features speakers from emergent territories to discuss their own stories and the obstacles they had to go through in order to become developers in places where the industry was still non-existent.

USA, 2019

Women in Gaming: 100 Professionals of Play

Featured as one of the 100 women in the game industry! This book, written by Meagan Marie and published by Prima Games celebrates women's accomplishments in the industry from all over the world, from women who helped establish it, to those who will someday lead it.

Chile, 2020

First Game to Win a Literary Award in Chile!

At the end of 2020 my game Nanopesos was awarded the Literary Award from the Ministry of Culture of Chile, marking a milestone as the first videogame in our country to win it, paving the way for more games to apply the following years.

2016 - Present

Event Speaker & Judge

Since 2016 I have taken part as a speaker and judge in several events around the world such as GDC, ACM SIGCHI, Technovation, among others. I love to help and inspire young people and those from emerging territories with what I have learned in my career!
